Columns

Here's the CSS for columns. They're on line 432 in the CSS file.

.row

All .column-#'s must go inside .row for the columns to work and be responsive.

.column-2

Makes a column 50% of your screen's width. Put two in a .row to get even columns.

.column-3

Makes a column 33% of your screen's width. Put three in a .row to get even columns.

.column-4

Makes a column 25% of your screen's width. Put four in a .row to get even columns. Or put mix and match with .column-2's to get uneven columns.
