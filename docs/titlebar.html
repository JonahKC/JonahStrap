Title Bar

Here's all CSS for setting up a top bar like the one on this site. If you're looking for the classes in the CSS file, they start at line 221.

.topbar

.toptitle and .topbutton must go inside .topbar for the bar to work and the CSS to not spread to the rest of the page..

.toptitle

Puts your page title in the top left corner, taking up 50% of screen width.

.topbutton

Puts your links or buttons opposite to your title
