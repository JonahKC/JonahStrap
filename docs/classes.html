Classes

Here's all the custom classes! If you're looking for them in the CSS file, they start at line 73.

.center

Centers content with text-align: center;.

.vertcenter-side

Vertically and horizontally centers content, flexing left to right.

.vertcenter-up

Vertically and horizontally centers content, flexing top to bottom.

.margin-centered

Centers content with margins.

.space

Makes <hr> elements clear to act as spacers.

.phonespace

Makes <hr> elements clear to act as spacers, but disappears on mobile.

.wideline

Makes <hr> elements 100% wide and fancy.

.thinline

Makes <hr> elements 75% wide and fancy.

.title

Makes text 3x size and clears text decoration.

.subtitle

Makes text 2x size and clears text decoration.

.highlight

Gives content an accented background color and some padding, and centers everything inside.

.box

Puts content in a nice little rounded box with a black border.
